Biography

Mr. Tao Zhao received his B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2012 and 2015, respectively. He is currently a PhD student in Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, Texas A&M University, College Station, Texas, United States. His current research interests include distributed systems, cloud-based systems, and cyber-physical systems.
